  • Electrical Power Consumption

    • The above chart shows how much energy is used by each state to perform an action such as making car parts or energy used to power your home. Natural Gas and Coal appears to be the most used energy source as of 2018. States like Connecticut, New Hampshire, and South Carolina use Nuclear Energy. Nuclear Energy powers over 50% of America’s Clean Energy, is more reliable, and does not emit greenhouse gases while generating electricity. In 2018 99% of Vermont’s energy consumption was Renewable energy.
